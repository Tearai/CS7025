Education Game

Studio: Team Gamma

Team Size: 7

My Primary Roles: Game Designer, Programmer, Level Designer

Engine: Unity

Description:For our third year we have to make a video game with a charity. We had to communicate with the charity to discuss what their vision of a video game would be. They wanted us to create a hub world and have many different mini games for the player.

What I contributed:I was tasked to design 8 different mini game with 2 of my teammates and had to program the player movement and 4 out of the 8 mini games. I have also designed the hub level layout to make sure it resembles the main street of Dublin.
